Prediction of Car Fuel Consumption

S. Boisgérault

Abstract

We produce a model that predicts a vehicle fuel consumption from its weight, with a estimated standard deviation smaller than 2 liters per 100 km. The model is based on the Auto-MPG dataset.

Model

Our model is: fuel consumption = 0.00899249 × vehicle weight − 0.90305387 where the fuel consumption is measured in liters per 100 km and the vehicle weight in kg.

Error Distribution

Our model is practically unbiased |mean| ≤ 10−14 and its standard deviation is std ≈ 1.815 < 2.0.

Dataset

Auto-mpg comes from Quinlan (1993).
